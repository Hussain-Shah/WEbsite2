About Shardible

P2P Marketplace

Shardible is a modern decentralized NFT marketplace where people can easily buy and sell any NFT tokens. Our team implements all smart contracts with a passion for safety and a decentralization spirit. All listings, bids and transfers occur on our unique smart contract on a chain so users can be sure that their funds and NFTs are safe and secure.

SaaS for NFT Projects

NFT smart contract factory, minting and token gating are just a part of Shardible. The platform provides comprehensive functionality and a set of tools which help creators, artists, and developers start their NFT projects. Our mission is to simplify NFT creation and enable it for everyone,
